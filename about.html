What I use to do

At one time I was a PhD physics dude who worked at Los Alamos and then at Stanford. Then ran away to join some people doing sportbug, one of the first GPS websites devoted to sporting activities and things slowly changed. We eventually after a few attempts came up with Geotrax. There we developed a GPS tracking system to catch bank robbers. Really cool stuff.
