Cycling Routes

This is a pet project of mine making use of Google development tools to build a website to designe road bike rides. You click on a map create your ride and it shows you the distance, elevation profile and directions. You can then download a GPX file to use for routing in a Garmin.

I am working on a iPhone app that will allow you to sync your route onto it and the use it to do the routing.

Check out the site »

Phone in My Blog

This is another website in progress. The idea is to use the iPhone messaging app to create a blog entry and then message it to the server where it posts a blog entry. You can use Siri to do voice to text for the blog entry and use the camera to take pictures for the blog. The blog also shows a location on a map taken from the enbedded GPS data in the picture.
